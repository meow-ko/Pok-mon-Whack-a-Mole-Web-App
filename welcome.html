Whack-a-Mole Adventure!

How to Play

  • Choose difficulty: Easy, Medium, or Hard
  • Catch Pokémon as they pop up from Poké Balls
  • Click on them before they disappear to earn points
  • Different Pokémon are worth different points
  • Watch out for Zubat - they'll end your game!
  • Green glow = Good Pokémon | Red glow = Zubat (avoid!)
  • Gold glow = Shiny (2x points!) or Legendary
